Airports

If you're flying in, San Luis Obispo is the closest airport to minimize drive time. Chances are, though, that there will be more flight & car rental availability with the larger airports. Below are the closest airports & distances away from Paso:

San Luis Obispo County Regional Airport (SBP) 31mi / 35min I-101N (map)
Monterey Regional Airport (MRY) 115mi / 1h 45min I-101S (map)
Norman Y Mineta San Jose International Airport (SJC) 161mi / 2h 22min I-101S (map)
San Francisco International Airport (SFO) 161mi / 2h 54min I-101S (map)
Los Angeles International Airport (LAX) 215mi / 3h 24min I-405N → I-5N → Hwy 46 West (map)
